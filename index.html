Projects

obVeos - Texas A&M Bike Share Optimization

Worked with my Senior Design team as well as the Texas A&M Transportation Institute to develop tools to optimize the rebalancing of the VeoRide bikeshare at Texas A&M University using bike trip data provided by the university.

Implemented visualization tools into the monorepo from the Texas A&M's GeoInnovation Service Center to give insight into the problematic areas of the bikeshare system for load rebalancing.

Developed regression analysis model using linear regression and random forest for load analysis given many features of time and location to verify need for rebalancing and aid in future development of a predictive model for rebalancing.

Critter Slayer - 2D RPG Demo

Critter Slayer is a monster-battle RPG demo designed to showcase features of Unity and provide the groundwork for a full-fledged RPG to be implemented. I developed the code, created art assets, and the music.

As the sole programmer of Critter Slayer, I used Unity to create the game. I implemented a movement system with collision detection and animations, NPC dialogue system, a decision making system, a shop with items, as well as a battle system for fighting NPCs' critters.
